Academics

At Future Life Academy and Junior School, we are dedicated to providing a comprehensive and dynamic education. Our curriculum follows the CBC (Competency-Based Curriculum) framework, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and emotional intelligence.

Our Academic Programs

ECDE

Our ECDE program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and character development. It is to ignite bright future for all!

Primary School

We use the designed curriculum to prepare students for a future of success both in academic excellence, co-curricular activities and leadership development.Achieving greatness through knowledge, where minds grow and hearts thrive.

Junior School

We use designed curriculum for empowering minds, unlock potential and create passion for learning experiences that develop our children in body, mind and spirit. Everyone is valued and grow to their full potential!
