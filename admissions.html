Admissions

We are excited to welcome new students to Future Life Academy and Junior School! Please read through the information below and fill out the form to apply for admission.

Admission Process

Our admission process is simple and straightforward:

  • Fill out the online application form below.
  • Submit the required documents (birth certificate, passport-size photo, academic reports).
  • Attend an interview and/or entrance test (if applicable).
  • Receive your admission letter and join our vibrant community!

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (Copy)
  • Passport-size Photograph (2 copies)
  • Academic Reports from Previous School
  • Proof of Residence
  • Completed Application Form (Below)
